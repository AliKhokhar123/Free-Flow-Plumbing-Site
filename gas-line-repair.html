Signs Your Gas Line Needs Repair

Gas leaks are serious, but knowing what to look for keeps your family safe. Call us if you notice:

A rotten egg smell

Hissing or whistling sounds near gas lines

Dead plants around outdoor gas lines

Higher gas bills with no explanation

Pilot lights that keep going out
